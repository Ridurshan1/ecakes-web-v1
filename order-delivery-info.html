Delivery & Pickup Information

Delivery Info

Delivery Zones: We currently deliver across the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) and Metro Vancouver.

Delivery Fees: Standard delivery is $15 CAD. Free delivery on all orders over $100 CAD.

Delivery Times: Deliveries are made Mon-Sat, between 10am - 6pm. We cannot guarantee specific delivery times.

Lead Time: A minimum of 48 hours notice is required for all delivery orders.

Pickup Info

Pickup Location: 123 Cake Ave, Toronto, ON, M5V 2H1. Please see map for directions.

Pickup Hours: You can pick up your order Mon-Sat, from 11am - 7pm.

Instructions: Please bring your order confirmation email (printed or on your phone) when you come to pick up.

Lead Time: A minimum of 24 hours notice is required for all pickup orders.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change or cancel my order? expand_more

Changes and cancellations are accepted up to 48 hours before the scheduled delivery/pickup time. Please contact our support team with your order number to make any adjustments.

Do you offer cakes for specific dietary needs? expand_more

We offer a selection of gluten-free and vegan cakes. Please check the product description for dietary information. While we take precautions, we cannot guarantee against cross-contamination for severe allergies.

What happens if no one is home for the delivery? expand_more

If the recipient is not available, our driver will attempt to contact them. If we cannot reach them, the cake will be returned to our shop. A re-delivery fee will apply. We are not responsible for cakes left unattended.
