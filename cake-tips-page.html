Find quick answers to common cake care questions.

Yes, most of our cakes can be frozen! For best results, wrap the cake slices tightly in plastic wrap, then a layer of aluminum foil, and place them in an airtight container. They can be frozen for up to 2 months. To thaw, leave them in the refrigerator overnight.

We recommend taking your cake out of the refrigerator about 1-2 hours before serving. This allows the buttercream and cake to soften to the perfect, flavourful consistency. The exact time may vary depending on the temperature of your room.

For multi-tiered or tall cakes, transportation requires extra care. Place the cake box on a flat, level surface, such as the floor of the passenger seat or the trunk of your car. Avoid placing it on a slanted car seat. Drive carefully, avoiding sudden stops and sharp turns.
