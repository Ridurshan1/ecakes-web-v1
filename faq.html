How can we help?

Find answers to your questions about our cakes, ordering, and delivery.

How do I place a custom cake order? expand_more
To place a custom order, please visit our 'Custom Cakes' page and fill out the inquiry form. We'll get back to you within 24-48 hours to discuss your design, flavors, and provide a quote. We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance.
What are your delivery zones in Canada? expand_more
We currently deliver within the Greater Toronto Area (GTA). For deliveries outside this zone, please contact us for a special arrangement. You can also opt for in-store pickup from our Toronto location.
Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options? expand_more
Yes! We offer a selection of delicious gluten-free and vegan cakes. Please check the product description for dietary information or use the filters on our 'Cakes' page to find them easily. Note that while we take precautions, our kitchen is not a certified allergen-free environment.
What is your policy on order modifications or cancellations? expand_more
For standard cakes, you can modify or cancel your order up to 72 hours before the scheduled pickup/delivery date for a full refund. For custom cakes, a non-refundable 50% deposit is required, and cancellations must be made at least 7 days in advance.
What payment methods do you accept? expand_more
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and Interac e-Transfer for online orders. All prices are listed in Canadian Dollars (CAD) and are subject to applicable taxes.

Can't find your answer?

Our team is here to help. Reach out to us with any questions you have.
